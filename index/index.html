About our Academy

TOSKA Academy is a creative arts institution based in Saint Petersburg, Russia, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of visual storytellers and innovative creators. Our programs in film, graphic design, and music production provide students with hands-on training, industry-relevant skills, and an environment that inspires artistic growth.

At TOSKA Academy, we empower young minds to explore their craft, develop their voice, and shape the future of the creative world.
